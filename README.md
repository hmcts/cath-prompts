# cath-prompts
These are the predefined categories and their prompts available through the prompt server to support CaTH development

## Delivery
**Summary:** Prompts for planning and managing delivery work, including prioritisation, roadmapping, vision setting, stakeholder communication, and risk management.
**Prompts (5):**
- Risk Reg
- Feature prioritisation matrix
- Stakeholder update
- Product roadmap
- Product vision statement
---
## Product requirements
**Summary:** Prompts for defining, refining, and communicating requirements and user-facing outputs, from user stories and acceptance criteria to journeys, screens, guidance, and notifications.
**Prompts (11):**
- Acceptance criteria generator
- Refine user story
- Turn requirements into first-draft content
- Citizen guidance page
- Email notification to Citizen
- Email notification to legal professional
- Legal-professional guidance
- Requirements breakdown
- Technical specification from user story
- Multi-screen Journey
- Screen content
---
## Quality assurance
**Summary:** Prompts for reviewing existing content against standards and producing prioritised, actionable recommendations.
**Prompts (1):**
- Review provided content
---
## Team management
**Summary:** Prompts supporting team processes and continuous improvement activities.
**Prompts (1):**
- Retrospective action plan
