# cath-prompts
These are the predefined categories and their prompts available through the prompt server to support CaTH development

Delivery
Summary: Prompts for planning and managing delivery work (prioritisation, roadmap/vision, stakeholder comms, risks).
Prompts:
		- Risk Reg
		- Feature prioritisation matrix
		- Stakeholder update
		- Product roadmap
		- Product vision statement

Product requirements
Summary: Prompts for defining and refining requirements and user-facing outputs (stories, AC, specs, screen/journey content, guidance, notifications).
Prompts:
		- Acceptance criteria generator
		- Refine user story
		- Turn requirements into first-draft content
		- Citizen guidance page
		- Email notification to Citizen
		- Email notification to legal professional
		- Legal-professional guidance
		- Requirements breakdown
		- Technical specification from user story
		- Multi-screen Journey
		- Screen content

Quality assurance
Summary: Prompts for reviewing content against standards and producing prioritised recommendations.
Prompts:
		- Review provided content

Team management
Summary: Prompts for team process and continuous improvement.
Prompts:
		- Retrospective action plan
